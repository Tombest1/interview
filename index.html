Our Approach.

The firm is aim at helping farmers get funding for there farmer as well as providing security for there product. The process also involves the securing of insurance to cover both product and equipment that will givers and partners such as banks, financial institution, other private bodies and individuals who will help fund the project.

What we hope to acheive.

People Engagement and Marketing.

The engagement process is to educate the farmers and youth on the benefit of being a part of the program as we will be giving out farm lands with clear boundaries and at the lowest rate, also there won't be need for initial payment so as not to scare the people and support needed such as seeds, equipment and the land will be provided. Payment will be installmental and it will start once the proceed are being sold. With the help of marketers, the product will also be taken beyond the boarders of kaduna and even internationally.

Ensuring a continuous food value chain.

Ensuring a continuous food value chain.

Government Partnership

Liaise with the state government on how to get farm land at the lowest possible rate of which the payment will be installmental so as to help encourage people to key into the program.

Insurance

Partner with the Nigerian Agricultural Insurance Corporation for security of farm produce and equipment from unforeseen crises or crop infection and attack.

Tech Support

Get Agro-tech companies to partner with us in other to provide information and tools based on real time research that will help the farmers get quality product with less stress.

People Engagement

Engage the farmers and create awareness for the youth to make them see the benefit in taking advantage of the opportunity instead of staying idle.

Finance

Collaborate with financial institution, NGOs, Philanthropist, Government and Individuals who will donate freely.

Marketing

Engage Agricultural Marketing companies such 360° Marketing Solutions, also to leverage on the government to patronise the scheme and get a global market as well.
